Simple Interface



Echo Publish plays nice with your current systems


It offers you a simple, secure, and well-documented API to invoke the merging of data with templates. Data can be in the form of XML or JSON documents, or even just POST parameters. Flexible "flow" definitions allow you to control exactly how the merge should be done, and what should be done with the merged documents. Data can contain embedded images, or links to images on the web.

Easy Templates



Echo Publish makes it easy to create powerful templates


Just link your Echo Publish account to your Google Drive account, and you can use all the power of the tools that Google and other providers offer to create the templates that your business needs. Include tags to insert and format data fields, and go further to make use of the huge range of templating controls offered by the Django templating language.

Quick Generation




Echo Publish makes lovely pdfs in seconds


Echo Publish makes beautiful documents in seconds. Each call to the service returns links to view or download the merged documents. PDFs, HTMLs, DOCXs, and other options are all available. You can even arrange for Echo Publish to archive the documents for you, or to use them in emails.
